How to Play:

One Player Mode:

  • Use the Up and Down arrow keys to move your paddle
  • Or use your mouse to control the paddle position
  • Hit the ball with your paddle to score points
  • Don't let the ball pass your paddle!
  • Ball speed increases as your score rises
  • Get to 10 points to win!

Two Player Mode:

  • Player 1 (Left): Use W and S keys to move up and down
  • Player 2 (Right): Use Up and Down arrow keys
  • First player to reach 10 points wins!
